バンヤンツリー釜山 オナーズメンバーシップ：最高級リゾートライフと資産価値を両立する戦略

2026年11月グランドオープン予定のバンヤンツリー釜山。全室プライベートプール付の贅沢な空間と、世界中で利用可能なメンバーシップ特典、そして資産としての価値。ディレクターKがその魅力を徹底解説します。

Banyan Tree Busan Honors Membership: Three Pillars of Excellence

  • The Ultimate Luxury: The only sanctuary in Korea to bear the prestigious Banyan Tree flagship brand, ensuring a world-class hospitality experience.
  • Unrivaled Privacy: An architectural masterpiece where every room is a suite featuring a private pool, providing a secluded haven away from the world.
  • Definitive Asset Value: A 100% shared ownership model (Title Deed) that guarantees lifetime membership with full rights for transfer and inheritance.

As Director K, I have spent years observing the evolution of Seoul’s high-end culture, yet it is the coastline of Busan that currently commands my undivided attention. Beyond the familiar glitz of Haeundae and the rhythmic tides of Songjeong lies the Yeonha-ri coast—a place where the horizon meets a new standard of living. Banyan Tree Busan, scheduled for its grand unveiling in November 2026, is not merely a new development; it is the arrival of a global icon that transcends the traditional hotel concept to offer a holistic lifestyle for the most discerning elite.

In my perspective, the true allure of Banyan Tree Busan lies in its refusal to compromise. While many projects claim the title of "luxury," Banyan Tree carries a legacy of intimacy and ecological harmony that is peerless. This is the first time the group’s most prestigious flagship brand has been applied in Korea, making it a singular opportunity for those who seek the pinnacle of the Banyan Tree Group’s portfolio.

The distinction is found in the details that cater to the five senses. Imagine a retreat where you never have to share a pool; every single unit is designed as an Ocean Terrace Pool Suite. This architectural choice ensures that your moments of repose remain entirely your own. Furthermore, the integration of the legendary Saffron restaurant, the sophisticated Vertigo rooftop bar, and the world-renowned Banyan Tree Spa creates an ecosystem of refinement that has never before existed on this scale within the peninsula. The 360-degree panoramic views of the Gijang Yeonha coast are not just scenery; they are a living masterpiece that changes with the light of the East Sea.

From a director’s viewpoint, the Honors Membership is less of a purchase and more of a strategic acquisition of a legacy asset. The membership is structured as a 100% shared ownership, meaning you hold the title deed just as you would for premium real estate. This ensures that your investment is protected and can be passed down through generations. Whether you choose the Integrated, Villa, or Penthouse type, the entry points ranging from 300 million to 2.1 billion KRW reflect the scarcity and prestige of the brand. For corporations, it serves as an incomparable tool for VIP hospitality, while for individuals, it is the ultimate signature of a life well-lived.

One cannot discuss Banyan Tree Busan without addressing its journey of resilience. The 2025 fire incident was a moment of profound trial, but it ultimately served to strengthen the project’s foundation. The transition to Ssangyong Engineering & Construction—a firm with an illustrious pedigree in luxury hospitality—ensures that the final structure will meet the highest global standards of safety and craftsmanship. This commitment to excellence, even in the face of adversity, confirms that Banyan Tree Busan is built on a foundation of integrity.

Furthermore, the BTPC (Banyan Tree Private Collection) benefits transform this membership into a global passport. Owners are not confined to the shores of Busan; they gain privileged access to over 115 resorts across 29 countries, including the serene temples of Kyoto, the turquoise waters of the Maldives, and the vibrant culture of Phuket. This global connectivity is what truly defines a high-end lifestyle in the modern era.

As we move through the second round of recruitment, the window for securing this lifestyle at its most rational valuation is narrowing. Once the initial offering concludes, the market will dictate a premium that reflects the resort’s completed splendor. I invite you to envision your future here—as a founding owner of a sanctuary that will be whispered about in the same breath as the world’s most exclusive destinations.
