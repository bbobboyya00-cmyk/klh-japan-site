## Privacy Policy

K-Life Hack (hereinafter referred to as "the Site") is a technical documentation platform dedicated to sharing backend architecture, Linux system administration, and automation framework logs. The Site holds the security and data protection of our visitors as a paramount operational standard.

The Site URL: https://klifehack.com/

### 1. Server Access Logs and Metrics
The Site utilizes standard server log files. These files automatically register parameters such as anonymous IP addresses, browser types, Internet Service Providers (ISP), date/time stamps, referring/exit pages, and total volume of structural clicks. These metrics are entirely decoupled from personally identifiable identifiers and are processed strictly for infrastructure monitoring, payload analysis, and runtime environment tuning.

### 2. Cookies and Web Beacons
The Site leverages standard browser cookies to stabilize interface configurations and optimize the rendering layout of core programming code blocks. Visitors retain full architectural control via their local browser settings to disable or selectively restrict cookie transmission.

### 3. Google AdSense and Third-Party Data Operations
Google, as a third-party vendor, operates tracking cookies to serve automated advertisements across the Site based on historical navigation signals. Visitors may permanently opt-out of personalized advertising modules by modifying their central Google Ad Settings infrastructure (https://adssettings.google.com/).

### 4. Traffic Analytics Pipeline (Google Analytics)
The Site integrates Google Analytics to evaluate traffic density and detect malicious ingestion anomalies. All telemetry data is harvested on an anonymous vector, operating strictly under automated non-aggregate data models.

By utilizing the technical document nodes and shell repository notes on the Site, you hereby acknowledge full structural alignment and explicit consent to the data processing terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.
